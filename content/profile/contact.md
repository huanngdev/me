## Tagline (recommended)

For a 0-1 yr fullstack engineer, the highest-leverage tagline pattern is **what you do + a hint of taste**. Outcome-focused taglines ("helped startups ship faster") ring hollow without metrics; personality-only taglines ("Lives in TypeScript") feel cute but skippable.

Recommended:

> **Fullstack engineer in Ho Chi Minh City. I build Next.js products in TypeScript — and occasionally wander into Sui blockchain.**

Variants to A/B mentally:

- "Fullstack engineer. Next.js, Node.js, and the occasional Move contract."
- "TypeScript fullstack developer. Web products by day, Sui hackathons on weekends."
- "Building web products from Ho Chi Minh City. Fullstack TypeScript. Hackathon-tested on Sui."

Pick after the rest of the page exists — the tagline reads differently once the surrounding content is in place.
